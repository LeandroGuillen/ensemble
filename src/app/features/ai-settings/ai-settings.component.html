Getting Started with Local AI

Ensemble supports local AI for privacy and offline use. We recommend Ollama for the best experience.

1. Install Ollama

Visit ollama.com and download Ollama for your platform.

2. Download a Model

Open a terminal and run:

ollama pull llama3.2

This will download a small, fast model perfect for character name generation.

3. Enable AI in Ensemble

Toggle "Enable AI Features" above and test the connection.
